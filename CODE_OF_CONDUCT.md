# Code of conduct

## Our standard

supy-wingspan is a Supy internal engineering project. Everyone who contributes — employees,
contractors, and collaborators — is expected to keep interactions professional, direct, and
respectful. We optimise for good engineering and honest feedback, not for being agreeable.

## Expected behaviour

- Critique code and ideas, not people. "This standard is ambiguous" — not "you don't understand the standard".
- Assume competence and good intent; ask before assuming a mistake.
- Keep discussion in the open (issues and PRs) so decisions are traceable.
- Respect the security policy: no secrets in issues, PRs, or commits.

## Unacceptable behaviour

Harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, and sharing others' private information
are not tolerated. Deliberately leaking credentials or bypassing the plugin's security controls is
a serious violation.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns to the maintainer at <abed@supy.io>. Reports are handled confidentially.
Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and comments that violate this code, and may
restrict repeat offenders from the project.
